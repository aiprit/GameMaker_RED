Welcome to the Room Editor!
To add an object to your room, click on your 
desired object in the list on the left and drag it
into the room. To configure more object-specific 
properties, double click on the the object and 
specify its parameters in the popup.
To delete an object, click on its instance in the
room preview and press "delete." To copy an object,
click on the object and press Ctrl+V or Command+V
to paste.
