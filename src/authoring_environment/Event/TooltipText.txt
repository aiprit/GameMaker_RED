Welcome to the Event Editor!
To add an action to your event, either
double click an action, from the left pane,
drag and drop an action from the left pane,
or select an action from the left pane and 
click the add button.
To rearrange the order of actions, drag and drop
the actions within the left pane.  
To delete an action from the current list of 
actions, select the action from the left pane and
click the delete button.
To cancel the process, click the cancel button.
To save your changes, click the save event button.
